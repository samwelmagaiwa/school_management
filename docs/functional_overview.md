# System Functional Overview

This document provides a comprehensive overview of the School Management System's functional modules. It is designed for stakeholders to understand the capabilities, workflows, and business value of the platform.

---

## 1. Student Information Management

**Goal**: Centralize student data for easy access and lifecycle management.

- **Student Profiles**: Stores personal details, photos, contact information, and parent/guardian links.
- **Admissions**: Streamlined process for admitting new students, generating unique Admission Numbers, and assigning them to classes.
- **Promotion & Graduation**: Tools to promote students to the next class or graduate them upon completion.
- **Class & Section Management**: Define classes (e.g., Form 1, Grade 5) and sections (Stream A, B) to organize the student body.

## 2. Academics & Timetabling

**Goal**: Organize the learning environment and schedule.

- **Subject Management**: Define subjects offered (Math, Science, etc.) and assign them to classes.
- **Timetables**: Create and view weekly class schedules. Ensures students and teachers know where to be and when.
- **Attendance (Student)**: Track daily student presence. Reports help identify chronic absenteeism early.

## 3. Examination & Grading

**Goal**: Assess and record student performance accurately.

- **Exam Management**: Schedule exams (Mid-Term, Final) for specific terms.
- **Marks Entry**: Teachers enter marks for their subjects. The system supports various grading scales.
- **Grading Systems**: define grade ranges (A, B, C) and remarks automatically based on scores.
- **Report Cards**: Generate automated marks sheets and report cards for printing or digital distribution.

## 4. Human Resources & Attendance (New)

**Goal**: Manage staff lifecycles, attendance, and leave to ensure operational continuity.

### A. Staff Management

- **Employee Database**: Complete records of teachers, admins, and support staff.
- **Roles & Departments**: Organize staff into departments (Science, Admin, Transport) with specific designations.

### B. Staff Attendance

- **Daily Tracking**: Mark staff as Present, Absent, Late, or Half-Day.
- **Self-Service**: Staff can view their own attendance history via the "My HR Portal".
- **Time Tracking**: Records Clock-In and Clock-Out times to monitor punctuality.

### C. Leave Management

- **Leave Requests**: Staff can apply for leave (Sick, Annual, Maternity) online.
- **Approval Workflow**: Administrators review and Approve/Reject requests.
- **Balance Tracking**: Monitors duration and type of leave taken.

### D. Payroll Readiness

- **Salary Data**: Stores basic salary information for each employee.
- **Payroll Export**: Generates a CSV export of active staff and their salaries for processing by external payroll systems.

## 5. Accounting & Finance

**Goal**: Manage school finances, fees, and expenses.

- **Fee Structure**: Define fees for different classes (Tuition, Transport, Lunch).
- **Payment Collection**: Record payments from students/parents and issue receipts.
- **Invoicing**: Generate invoices for term fees.
- **Expenses**: Track operational expenses (utilities, supplies) to monitor cash flow.

## 6. Library Management

**Goal**: Track book inventory and lending.

- **Book Catalog**: Database of all books with ISBN, author, and category details.
- **Circulation**: Check-out and Check-in books for students and staff.
- **Overdue Tracking**: Alerts for books not returned by the due date.

## 7. Inventory & Store

**Goal**: Manage physical assets and supplies.

- **Item Manager**: Track stock of school supplies (chalk, paper, desks).
- **Requisitions**: Staff can request items from the store.
- **Stock Updates**: Record new stock arrivals and issuances to maintain accurate counts.

## 8. Transport Management

**Goal**: Manage school fleet and student transportation.

- **Vehicle Management**: Track school buses/vans and their details.
- **Routes & Trips**: Define transport routes and assign students to specific buses.

## 9. Hostel & Dormitory

**Goal**: Manage boarding facilities.

- **Dorm Allocation**: Assign students to specific dorms, rooms, and beds.
- **Occupancy Tracking**: Monitor bed availability and prevent double-booking.

## 10. System Administration

**Goal**: Secure and configure the platform.

- **User Roles & Permissions**: Granular control over what users (Teachers, Accountants, Parents) can see and do.
- **Settings**: Configure school information, current session/term, and system preferences.
- **Activity Logs**: Audit trails of who did what in the system for security and accountability.
